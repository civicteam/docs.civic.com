## What is Civic Nexus?

**Civic Nexus connects your AI assistant to all your tools** - GitHub, Slack, Google Drive, databases, and 50+ other services. Your AI can finally do real work instead of just answering questions.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit nexus.civic.com">
    Browse and select from 50+ tools and services you want to connect at **[nexus.civic.com](https://nexus.civic.com)**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Your Connection">
    Get your MCP URL or install command
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your AI Client">
    Paste the URL or run the install command in your AI assistant
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Your Tools">
    When you first use a tool, authorize access directly in the chat interface
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using">
    Ask your AI to use your connected tools in plain English
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**No server management, no API keys to juggle, no complex configurations.**
</Note>
