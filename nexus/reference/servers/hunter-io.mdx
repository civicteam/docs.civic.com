## Overview

The Hunter.io server enables AI assistants to interact with [Hunter.io](https://hunter.io), providing professional email finding, verification, and domain research capabilities with confidence scoring and comprehensive contact discovery.

## How to Add Hunter.io

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Hunter.io Account">
    1. Go to [https://hunter.io](https://hunter.io)
    2. Sign up for a free account or log in to your existing account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate API Key">
    1. Navigate to your account dashboard
    2. Go to **API** section
    3. Copy your API key
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Nexus">
    1. Add the Hunter.io server to your Nexus environment
    2. Enter your API key when prompted
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Connection">
    Start with a simple command like "How many searches do I have left this month?" to verify the connection works properly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The free tier provides 25 domain searches and 50 email verifications per month, which reset on the last day of each month.
</Tip>

## Use Cases

### Credit Monitoring
"How many searches and verifications do I have left this month?" - Track your API usage and plan limits.

### Email Discovery
"Find the email address for John Kelly at acme.com" - Locate specific contacts at target companies.

### Executive Outreach
"Get me Patrick Collison's email at stripe.com" - Find decision-makers by name and company.

### Email Verification
"Verify if john.doe@example.com is a valid email address" - Validate emails before adding to your CRM.

### Domain Research
"Search for all emails at dropbox.com and show me the first 5 results" - Discover all available contacts at a company.

### Department Targeting
"Find marketing department emails at adobe.com" - Target specific departments for outreach campaigns.

<Note>
**Free Tier Constraints:**
- 25 domain searches per month (resets on the last day)
- 50 email verifications per month
- 50 credits total available
- No access to bulk operations or API integrations on free tier

**Accept-all servers:** Some domains accept all emails at SMTP level, making verification less reliable. Hunter flags these with confidence scores.
</Note>

<Warning>
**Known Limitations:**
- **No bulk exports** - Free tier limited to single queries (no CSV exports)
- **Rate limits** - API calls are throttled to prevent abuse
- **Historical data** - Cannot access email discovery history or saved searches on free tier
</Warning>
