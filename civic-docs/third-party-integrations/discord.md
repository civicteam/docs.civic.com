# Discord

With the [<mark style="color:purple;">Civic Pass Discord Bot</mark>](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client\_id=984369541108875327\&permissions=2415937536\&redirect\_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fapi.civic.com%2Fdiscord%2Fregister\&response\_type=code\&scope=bot%20identify%20applications.commands), members of your Discord server can gain special roles based on the Civic Passes they have in their Solana wallet.

#### Integration takes 1 minute...

1. Invite the Discord bot by clicking this link:  [<mark style="color:purple;">**`Civic Pass Discord Bot`**</mark>](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client\_id=984369541108875327\&permissions=2415937536\&redirect\_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fapi.civic.com%2Fdiscord%2Fregister\&response\_type=code\&scope=bot%20identify%20applications.commands)
2. Direct your users to use the `/link` command and click on the button that is created.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (26).png>)

Users will be asked to connect their wallet to their Discord account.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (35).png>)

After which, they will be allocated roles based on their passes.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (32).png>)

Done!

#### Next steps...&#x20;

Create private channels and grant access to the roles you create, in order to allow special access to pass-holders.
