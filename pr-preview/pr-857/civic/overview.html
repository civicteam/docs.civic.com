Welcome to Civic

What is Civic?​

Civic is the agent integrator for mid-market businesses. We ship forward deployed agents — production agents scoped to your workflow, built on your stack, governed from day one.

The Civic platform connects your AI agents to all your tools — GitHub, Slack, Dropbox, databases, CRMs, and dozens of other services — with guardrails, audit trails, and instant revocation built in.

Our team has shipped production systems in financial services, legal identity, enterprise AI, manufacturing, and publishing for twenty years. We bring that operational depth to every agent we deploy.

The Civic Method​

  1. 1
    Discovery

    45-minute call, free. Pick one workflow your team keeps postponing — RFP response, pitch prep, contract review, AP coding, RFQ response. You walk out with a one-page scope memo.

  2. 2
    Scope & Build

    On your stack. Built on your data, your SSO, your infrastructure. Civic components only where you want them or where nothing you already run does the job.

  3. 3
    Shadow

    Human-in-the-loop validation. Your team reviews every agent action before it runs autonomously. Configurable review checkpoints. No change orders unless you expand scope.

  4. 4
    Cutover

    A live production agent, a clear view of what it's doing, documentation to manage it, and a full handover if you want to run it yourself.

Organize Tools with Toolkits​

Create focused groups of tools for specific workflows instead of overwhelming your AI with every available service.

note

Why toolkits matter: When AI assistants have access to too many tools at once, they often choose the wrong ones or get confused. Toolkits solve this by creating focused, task-specific tool groups.

Marketing Analytics

Google Analytics + Notion + Slack for pulling data, documenting insights, and sharing reports

Developer Debugging

GitHub + PostgreSQL + Notion for investigating issues, querying databases, and documenting solutions

Governance & Security​

Every agent deployed through Civic runs inside a governance envelope: scoped access per task, tamper-evident audit trail, one-click kill switch, and SOC 2 Type 2-aligned controls.

note

Your data security is our top priority. Civic is designed for secure infrastructure with encrypted credential management and proactive server security reviews.

Audit Trail

Every tool call logged — tool name, parameters, response, timestamp, agent identity

Guardrails

Block tools, enforce read-only access, preset parameters at tool, server, and toolkit level

Revocation

Instant revocation at any granularity — one tool, one server, or the entire toolkit
