🎯 Two Ways to Consume Credits

Civic Chat — Use Civic with its LLM via the chat interface. Includes full reasoning and orchestration cost. Best for prototyping, testing integrations, and iterating on multi-step logic.

Your Own Tooling — Use Civic in your chatbot, IDE, or SDK. Credits measure only Civic orchestration, not your LLM costs. Best for embedding Civic in apps and automating workflows programmatically.

📊 What's Included in a Credit?

Credits measure end-to-end execution cost including LLM compute, tool/API calls (Slack, HubSpot, GitHub, etc.), data handling, and SDK-driven execution.

⚠️ Credits Do Not Roll Over

Everything expires at the end of your billing cycle, including if you cancel.
