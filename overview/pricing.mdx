<Note>
**How MAU limits work for web3 apps:**

Web3 apps with embedded wallets get BOTH limits:
- **15,000 MAUs** for users who authenticate without creating a wallet
- **2,000 MAUs** for users who create wallets

After reaching 2,000 users with wallets, new wallet creations will be throttled, but users without wallets can still authenticate as long as the 15,000 MAU limit is not exceeded.
</Note>
