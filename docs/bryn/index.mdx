**Bryn is the Signal-Based GTM agent for Growth teams.**

Bryn watches anonymous and known product, web, and intent signals, names the
pattern, and runs the Play you approved, with the audit log attached.

In practice: Bryn tells you which companies are on your website, understands
what they are doing there, and acts on it inside rules you set. A good-fit
account keeps returning to your pricing page, and your sales channel hears
about it in Slack with the evidence attached. The same identification can
update your CRM or personalize the page the visitor is looking at.
