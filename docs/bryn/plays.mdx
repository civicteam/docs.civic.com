Identification without action is a dashboard you will stop checking. Plays
are how Bryn acts on what it sees: automated workflows that fire when an
account does something that matters, inside rules you set once.

## Anatomy of a Play

<ThemedImage
  alt="Every Play is a trigger, conditions, and actions, with a review gate before anything sends."
  sources={{
    light: '/images/bryn/d5-play-anatomy-light.svg',
    dark: '/images/bryn/d5-play-anatomy-dark.svg',
  }}
/>

- **Trigger**: what the account did. Signals and score thresholds, like
  `pricing_page_viewed` plus a climbing intent score.
- **Conditions**: who it applies to. Your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP),
  trial state, account attributes.
- **Actions**: what happens. Alert Slack, draft outreach, update the CRM.
  Outbound actions can hold for a human review first.

## Enable your first Play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plays">
    In the Bryn dashboard, go to **Plays**. Each Play shows its trigger and
    what it will do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the rules">
    Check the trigger and conditions read the way you want. The signals it
    references come from [your mapping](/bryn/signal-mapping).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch it on">
    Flip the toggle. A Play starts or stops right away, and nothing runs
    while it is off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**You'll know it worked when** the Play shows as active, and the first
matching account produces its action: watch **Activity** for the run and
your Slack channel for the alert.

## Stay in control

Autonomy inside bounds is the whole design. Every control is a feature you
can point your reviewers at:

- **Nothing sends without you.** Plays with outbound actions hold the run
  for review: the held run shows in **Activity** with an **Unhold** button,
  or an approval link in Slack.
- **The kill switch is a single click.** **Pause this Play** stops one;
  **Pause all Plays** stops everything at once, and resuming is just as
  fast.
- **Every action is on the record.** The **Audit log** captures every
  signal, score, match, and execution: your work record, and the same
  trail your CFO and DPO inherit.
- **Cooldowns** stop the same Play re-firing on the same account, a week
  by default.
