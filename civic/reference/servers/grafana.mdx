## Overview

The Grafana server provides access to Grafana's full observability stack, including dashboards, Prometheus metrics, Loki logs, Tempo traces, Pyroscope profiles, alert rules, incidents, and OnCall scheduling. Use it to query, visualize, and manage your monitoring infrastructure from a single interface.
