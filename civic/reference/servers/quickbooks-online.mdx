## Overview

QuickBooks Online is cloud accounting software for managing your chart of accounts, customers, vendors, invoices, bills, and payments. Connect it to Civic to let your AI agent handle bookkeeping tasks and generate financial reports.

<Card title="Add to Civic" icon="plus" href="https://nexus.civic.com">
  Connect QuickBooks Online at nexus.civic.com
</Card>

## Available Tools (9)

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `accounts` | Get and list QuickBooks chart of accounts |
| `bills` | Manage QuickBooks bills (create, get, list, update, delete) |
| `customers` | Manage QuickBooks customers (create, get, list, update, delete) |
| `employees` | Manage QuickBooks employees (create, get, list, update) |
| `invoices` | Manage QuickBooks invoices (create, get, list, update, delete) |
| `items` | Manage QuickBooks items — inventory, service, and non-inventory (create, get, list, update, delete) |
| `payments` | Manage QuickBooks payments (create, get, list, update, delete) |
| `reports` | Generate QuickBooks financial reports (balance sheet, P&L, cash flow, aged receivables, and more) |
| `vendors` | Manage QuickBooks vendors (create, get, list, update, delete) |
