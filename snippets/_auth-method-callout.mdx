<Note>
**How authentication works**

Civic supports two methods depending on how your client connects:

| Client type | Method | How it works |
|---|---|---|
| Interactive clients (Claude Desktop, Cursor, VS Code, Gemini CLI, Goose, Windsurf) | **OAuth** | When you connect, your client opens a browser window to nexus.civic.com. Sign in once — no manual token needed. |
| Automated agents (LangChain, custom scripts, OpenAI SDK, Anthropic SDK) | **Civic Token** | Generate a bearer token at nexus.civic.com → Settings → Tokens. Pass it as an `Authorization: Bearer` header. |

See [Get Your Credentials](/civic/quickstart/credentials) for full details.
</Note>
